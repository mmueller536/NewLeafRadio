Original Composer: Kazumi Totaka

These tracks are from Animal Crossing: New Leaf and their existance is in thanks to the work by original composer Kazumi Totaka. For more information on contributions Kazumi has played in Animal Crossing as well as various other Nintendo intellectual products click here!

Music Modified and Extended by: JezDayy

Rather than rip the loopable tracks straight from the game, their mp3's were gathered from the youtube channel Jez run by JezDayy. Seriously check out their channel. Their work involves way more than just extending Animal Crossing audio and I've personally checked out their channel in the past for music to use for studying (especially the raining tracks).

I plan to never feature their personal works here as I would hate to steal their work and rob them of any monetization that comes from watching works that they have personally compiled/composed themselves. What will be here are just the extended tracks featured from their channel.
