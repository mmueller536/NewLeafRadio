Where's the music?

We'd like to just automatically start playing your favorite idle music for you right away, but some browsers have taken issue to the autoplay functionality as it can be very disruptive and annoying to someone browsing the web to stumble on a suddenly very noisy site.

So if you come on in here and want to listen to some music, just click below and the radio will hum to life before your very eyes! Should you want to take a quick break from the music (because your dog needs to go out, in-laws are calling, or need a quick coffee break), just click below again and our radio will wait patiently for your return.
